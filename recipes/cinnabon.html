Cinnabon Recipe



Description

This Cinnabon recipe will guide you through making the famous cinnabon cinnamon rolls from the mall!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk (110째F/45째C)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, warm the milk and sprinkle the yeast over it. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add the sugar, melted butter, salt, eggs, and flour to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place it in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).
  5. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a large rectangle (about 16x12 inches).
  6. Spread the softened butter over the dough. In a small bowl, mix the brown sugar and cinnamon together, then sprinkle it evenly over the buttered dough.
  7. Starting from the long edge, tightly roll up the dough into a log. Cut it into 12 equal pieces and place them in a greased baking dish.
  8. Cover and let the rolls rise for another 30 minutes.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. While the rolls are baking, prepare the frosting by mixing together powdered sugar, cream cheese, butter, vanilla extract, and milk until smooth.
  11. Once the rolls are done baking, spread the frosting over them while they are still warm.
  12. Serve and enjoy your homemade Cinnabon cinnamon rolls!
